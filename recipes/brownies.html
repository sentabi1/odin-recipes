Brownies

Description

A chocolate brownie or simply a brownie is a square or rectangular chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin" on their upper crust.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Walnut Halves

Steps

  1. Step 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Step 2.Combine the melted butter, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, eggs, flour, baking powder, and salt. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnut halves, if desired.
  3. Step 3Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with crumbs, not wet. Cool on wire rack.
